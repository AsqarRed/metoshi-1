What is Metoshi Platform?

METOSHI is a one-stop entertainment platform that combines a Comics Studio, Metoshi Exchange, and Metoshi GameFI Studio. Also, in the first quarter of 2022, its own marketplace and Metoshi DEX will be launched.

What is a Governance NFT?

It is a non-fungible token based on blockchain technology proving that you own a one-of-a-kind digital art piece / identity people can buy and trade. Furthermore, Governance NFTs grant the right to co-own the Metoshi Platform, earn dividends & vote, as well as other perks of the Ecosystem

Are all Governance NFTs the same?

All Governance NFTs are different in content and different in characteristics. Their minting is performed randomly. Everyone has a chance to get a Rare, Super Rare, or Special Governance NFT.

What are the advantages of Governance NFTs?

Governance NFTs offers the following advantages:
1) Co-ownership of Metoshi Platform and the right to vote on key decisions
2) The right to earn monthly dividends on your NFTs
3) Reduced or zero fees when trading on Metoshi CEX
4) Using Governance NFTs, you can farm Game, Collectible and Comics NFTs from Metoshi

How can I get a Governance NFT?

At the first stage, you've got a unique chance to purchase Governance NFTs on the cheap at Seed Round and Pre-sale. You will need to connect your Metamask browser extension to Metoshi Platform. Then follow the instructions

How can I sell Governance NFTs?

Governance NFTs can be sold in the secondary market - using our own marketplace, NFTrade or BakerySwap Marketplace

What blockchain does Metoshi use?

Binance Smart Chain. It is a blockchain that enables scalable, secure, and instant transactions. For the first and second quarters of 2022, cross-chains with Ethereum and Polygon are envisaged
